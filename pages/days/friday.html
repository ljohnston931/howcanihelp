Activities On Fridays:

Gym Kids

11:00 am

Participate in gym activities with children who have special needs to help them develop their cognitive, motor, and social skills. Help the students develop autonomy by doing what they want to do, including their creativity.


Utah Healing Arts

2:40pm

Utah Healing Arts performs for residents of local nursing homes. Volunteers share their talents through music, dance, art, theater, or by interacting with the audience members. Each event lasts about an hour. Volunteers choose what and how many events to attend. Meet at Jamba Juice in the Wilkinson Center for carpooling.


Kids Respite Care

6:00 pm

Friday's Kids Respite is a program where students watch children with disabilities on Friday or Saturday nights to allow their parents to have a break.
