Activities On Thursdays:

Gym Kids

11:00 am

Participate in gym activities with children who have special needs to help them develop their cognitive, motor, and social skills. Help the students develop autonomy by doing what they want to do, including their creativity.


Youth Detention Center

6:00 pm

The Youth Detention Center program provides mentors for the youth at the Slate Canyon Youth Detention Center located in Provo, Utah. We currently provide reading tutoring, a book club, mentoring, sports recreation, and a bi-weekly life skills class to youth aged 13 to 18. Volunteers are role models and friends to the youth. Volunteers must be at least 21 years of age and receive a background check after their first visit. Come help inmates learn to read Tuesdays and Thursdays from 6-7 pm or play sports Saturday mornings.
