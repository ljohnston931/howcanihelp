Activities On Tuesdays:

Bowling

3:10 pm

Come meet us at Miracle Bowl to socialize with the special needs community of RAH! Meet at the bottom of the LSB at 3:10 to carpool.


Vineyard at BYU

5:30 pm

Vineyard at BYU does online service for the LDS Church in a social setting. Bring your computer and come serve with us! More details on our Facebook page.


Youth Detention Center

6:00 pm

The Youth Detention Center program provides mentors for the youth at the Slate Canyon Youth Detention Center located in Provo, Utah. We currently provide reading tutoring, a book club, mentoring, sports recreation, and a bi-weekly life skills class to youth aged 13 to 18. Volunteers are role models and friends to the youth. Volunteers must be at least 21 years of age and receive a background check after their first visit. Come help inmates learn to read Tuesdays and Thursdays from 6-7 pm or play sports Saturday mornings.


Medallion Manor

6:15 pm

BYU Medallion Manor is a program where a group of volunteers make weekly visits to Medallion Manor, homes for adults with special needs in south Provo and Springville, on Tuesday and Wednesday evenings but during the Spring and Summer terms we will only meet on Tuesday evenings.
